 - 1 1-lb. Steak
 - 1 cup diced mushrooms
 - 1/4 cup chopped onion
 - 1/4 cup sliced carrots
 - Bulgogi Sauce

Directions:

1. Slice steak into slivers.  Place steak bits, mushrooms, carrots, and onions in a sealable container. Add bulgogi marinade until all the food is covered. Store in refrigerator overnight.

2. Drain excess sauce from mixture and throw on pre-heated grill pad. Cook until meat is done.
